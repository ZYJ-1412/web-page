About us

Furry Friends Paradise is a site mainly devoted to furry animals. Here, you can share your life with furry friends through photos, videos, drawings and so on. Our aim is to fight against discrimination and cruelty to animals. It's a place where enjoying time with furry friends relives stress and loneliness.
